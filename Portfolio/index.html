About me

Who is Jeremy Whiteside?

Do to others as you would have them do to you is my motto in life. Jesus was the greatest teacher, and I strive to be like him. For this reason, I live to help others. Helping people gives me a feeling of satisfaction that nothing else can. Through helping people, I want to make the world a better place. While I probably won't find a cure for a disease, or end any wars in my lifetime, I am certain that I will have made a positive difference in many people's lives. When I die I safely say that I contributed to society and didn't make any enemies.

Finding creative solutions to problems also brings me much satisfaction. I have always loved building things, solving puzzles, and math. Programming combines these three activities. I am thankful that I can pursue something that brings me happiness as a career, and am fortunate enough to have discovered it early

I am also passionate about music. I have a strong base in piano, but have also picked up a few other instruments along the way. I completed my grade 7 piano certification with the Royal Conservatory of Music in 2012, and then shifted my focus towards electronic music, which I now compose in my spare time.

My projects

Look what I made!

Here are a few projects that I have worked on in the past

Milleyvolts

Milleyvolts is a website that I made for an electrical contractor working in the Simcoe County area. He wanted something very simple and modest. It was made over the course of summer 2014 as a project to keep me occupied, and to make some extra money.
It is still in the making, and will be updated as the client provides me with content.

×

Arduino musical keyboard

This is an arduino musical keyboard that I made for my Computer Architecture course for my final project.
I bought a kids musical keyboard for a couple dollars off kijiji, and gutted it; I only needed the keys. I then wired the keys to my arduino which is running a custom program that I designed. All the notes are playable, and the keys all play the correct notes.

×

Ip man - Movie poster re-creation

This is a poster that I made for an assignment in my Intro to Web Design class. I had to re-create a movie poster using html and css code. I chose Ip Man because it is an awesome movie, a must see if you are a fan of kung-fu movies.
