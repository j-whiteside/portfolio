About me

Who is Jeremy Whiteside?

Do to others as you would have them do to you is my motto in life. Jesus was the greatest teacher, and I strive to be like him. For this reason, I live to help others. Helping people gives me a feeling of satisfaction that nothing else can. Through helping people, I want to make the world a better place. While I probably won't find a cure for a disease, or end any wars in my lifetime, I am certain that I will have made a positive difference in many people's lives. When I die I safely say that I contributed to society and didn't make any enemies.

Finding creative solutions to problems also brings me much satisfaction. I have always loved building things, solving puzzles, and math. Programming combines these three activities. I am thankful that I can pursue something that brings me happiness as a career, and am fortunate enough to have discovered it early My dad is the reason why I love technology. He is a computer programmer, and sparked my interest in computers at a very young age.

I am also passionate about music. I have a strong base in piano, but have also picked up a few other instruments along the way. I completed my grade 7 piano certification with the Royal Conservatory of Music in 2012, and then shifted my focus towards electronic music, which I now compose in my spare time.
